The Tree That I Planted

Title description-MY Type-POEM

When I was upset,
or in pain,
Or everything I did went in wain,
You were always there for me.

When dad beat me,
Mom didn't talk,
Without even a 'Hello', everyone away walked,
You were present to talk.

I could share with you,
My sorrows, My dreams,
The secretly eaten Ice-Creams,
You didn't shout or get bored.

Without a frowning,
You listened to my poor jokes,
About Maths, Food and stupid folks,
You didn't walk away from me.

You're My, No! Everbody's,
Best Friend ever Wanted,
Oh! Dear Tree that I planted,
You're my Bestest Friend!

Its true whats said,
People leave in times bad,
You'll remain ever, Happy or Sad,
My Dear Tree that I planted.
