Do The Right Things At Right Time

Title description-None Type-POEM

Mom always shouted,
Shut down the T.V.,
Child have some fears,
Your exams are Near.

Dad always said,
Stop playing games,
Open your textbooks and start Studying,
Your exams are papers are Rushing.

Dad draged my Football away,
Mom shutdown T.V.,
So,frustated took my TextBooks,
which were inside bag tied on a hook.

I was very Angry,
Mind Hot and Irritated,
I'm a Smarty,I'm Ready,
Why do they always tell me to Study.

At the Day of Exams,
I understood thier concerns,
The 'Fill in the Blanks' were scaring,
and the 'True or False' was Roaring.

Answers I did not know,
As I had always,
Wasted my Precious Time in,
T.V.,Games,Hanging out and enjoyin'.

That day 2 very important ,
Lessons I learnt,
Listen to elders, What they Say,
Right Things at Right Times or you'll have to Pay!
