About COEP

Title description, Dec 30,2016

College of Engineering, Pune (COEP) is an autonomous engineering college affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Established in 1854, it is one of the oldest engineering colleges in Asia, after IIT Roorkee (1847). The students and alumni of College of Engineering, Pune are colloquially referred to as COEPians. The college's study model was referred to, in the early 1950s, as the "Poona Model". College of Engineering, Pune is located at the confluence of the Mula river and Mutha river..
