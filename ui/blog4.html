Title description, Dec 30,2016

I don't know how the thought,
Crossed my Mind,
The Cook-Book ,
I started to Find.

I dreamt of,
Making a Cake,
For making that,
I had to Bake.

Mom said,
'Its good',
'To try something New,'
'Like learning to Cook'.

But First,
You need to Learn,
The Basic things,
turn-by-Turn.

Have a Experienced One,
for you to Supervise,
Child do not be foolish,
Be Wise.

I didn't listen to her,
As I was with full energy Wild,
and in a hurry,
to prove me a Smart Child.

But I forgot,
What said Dad,
Not listening to your Elders,
and being oversmart Goes Bad.

OverConfident Me,
In the flow of proving Myself,
To get all ingredients,
I opened the Shelf.

I didn't realize,
I was being OverConfident,
Silly Me thinking I'm,
the most Intelligent!

Day-Dreaming I would make a Cake,
which would praised by All,
In Lack of Awareness, Instead of adding powdered Sugar,
I mixed Salt which looked same White Small.

The cake which,
Was supposed to be Sweet,
Became in Over-Confidence,
A Salty Treat...

That Day A lesson I learnt,
Experts are not made in one Run,
Slowly learning,
and practice steps turn-by-turn

I was sad and sat crying,
Mom smiled,'You realized your Mistake',
'No problem Dear, we will',
'Together make a Amazing Choclate Cake!'

Its Me Gaurav

Just me, myself and I, exploring the universe of unknownment. I have a heart of love and a interest of Knowledge. I want to share my world with you.
